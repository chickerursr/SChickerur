01 · About

Researcher · Educator · Mentor

I am a Professor at KLE Technological University and Director of the Centre for Artificial Intelligence Research. I am an NVIDIA DLI Certified Instructor and Ambassador, bringing cutting-edge GPU programming knowledge to learners across India and beyond.

My work spans high-performance computing, deep learning, quantum machine learning, and applied AI — from theoretical research to hands-on industry training at IIT Bombay, IIT Palakkad, NIT Calicut, and international venues in China and the USA.

I am the founder of TechXcelerate Innovations Pvt. Ltd. — an AI-focused startup specialising in healthcare AI, quantum computing, and intelligent product design.
